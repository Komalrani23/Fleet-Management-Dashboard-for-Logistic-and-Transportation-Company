**Insights Drawn from the Dashboard**

•	Analyzing average order weight, total distance traveled, fuel usage, and maintenance costs enables the company to optimize operational efficiency. 

•	By identifying opportunities for load optimization, route planning, and fuel efficiency improvements, the company can reduce transportation costs, improve resource utilization, and enhance overall operational performance.

•	The analysis of total orders provides insights into peak order periods and fluctuations in demand.

•	This information allows the company to optimize staffing and resource allocation to efficiently meet customer needs during high-demand periods, ensuring customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

•	Monitoring total net revenue offers valuable insights into the company's financial performance. 

•	By analyzing revenue trends and seasonality effects, the company can make informed decisions regarding pricing strategies, service offerings, and revenue optimization to maximize profitability.

•	Monitoring total fuel costs, maintenance costs, and fixed costs allows the company to control operating expenses and identify opportunities for cost savings. 

•	Implementing fuel-efficient driving practices, preventive maintenance programs, and strategic cost-saving measures helps optimize cost management and improve profitability. 

•	Visualizations such as the top 10 cities with the most number of orders and highest net revenue generated provide valuable customer insights.
